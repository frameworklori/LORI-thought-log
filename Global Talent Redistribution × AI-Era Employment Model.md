Global Talent Redistribution × AI-Era Employment Model


⸻

ENGLISH VERSION

1. Problem Context

Some countries have advanced AI; some do not.
This creates global inequality in productivity, but also creates a new opportunity:

AI-rich countries have surplus talent;
AI-poor countries have labor gaps.

This mismatch can be transformed into a mutual-benefit system instead of a crisis.

⸻

2. The Solution: Global Talent Redistribution (GTR Model)

A structured model where:

**(A) U.S. young talent gets retrained →

(B) Is matched with foreign markets lacking AI →
(C) Works remotely or hybrid to support industries →
(D) Creates mutual value flow back to the U.S.**

This solves two problems simultaneously:
	•	U.S. new graduates get jobs
	•	Other countries get high-skill support they lack

It becomes a global win-win labor ecosystem.

⸻

3. Creator-Based Employment Model

Among every generation, there is always:
	•	10–20% creators (idea generators, system designers, innovators)
	•	20–30% builders (coding talent, engineers, implementers)
	•	50% operators (maintenance, execution, service roles)

brilliant system:

Let the top creators among young graduates:
	•	generate concepts
	•	build new application ideas
	•	propose new business models
	•	design new solutions

Then match them with:
	•	coding engineers
	•	software teams
	•	international partners

to convert ideas → reality.

This creates new employment teams, not just individual jobs.

⸻

4. Three-Layer AI-Era Team Structure

To stabilize employment, we can formalize this into a 3-layer job-creation architecture:

Layer 1 — Creators (Idea Layer)
	•	Identify talented young innovators
	•	Assign them to ideation labs
	•	Generate product/service concepts

Layer 2 — Builders (Code & Engineering Layer)
	•	Software engineers
	•	AI coders
	•	System integrators
	•	Designers

They execute and convert ideas into working products.

Layer 3 — Operators (Global Talent Layer)
	•	Remote support workers
	•	Industry-specific staff
	•	Local partners in foreign countries
	•	Service & maintenance roles

These roles absorb large numbers of graduates.

Thus, 1 idea → 3 teams → 6–20 jobs.
This is exponential job-generation, not replacement.

⸻

5. Why this model works ethically and economically

(1) Prevents AI-driven unemployment

Because AI doesn’t replace,
AI reorganizes human talent into layered value.

(2) Restores dignity to young graduates

Creators lead innovation.
Builders maintain technical flow.
Operators expand global value.
Everyone has a role.

(3) Benefits AI-poor countries

They get high-level U.S. skills without needing full AI.

(4) Strengthens U.S. competitiveness

Talent export ≠ loss
→ It becomes influence, soft power, and economic leverage.

(5) Creates sustainable global value chains

Countries with weak AI infrastructure still gain:
	•	digital transformation
	•	industry upgrades
	•	remote service capacity

And U.S. job market becomes stabilized.

⸻

6. LORI View — Structural Principle

AI should not centralize value;
it should redistribute opportunity through human talent.

The countries with AI have a responsibility to uplift those without it,
but cannot sacrifice their own young generation.
Therefore: global value must be built by structured teams, not machines alone.

LORI Framework strengthens this through:

(A) Global Talent Equilibrium Index (GTEI)

衡量各國人才缺口與美國人才剩餘。

(B) Creator-Build-Operator Model (CBO Model)

三層團隊架構吸收大量新鮮人。

(C) International Value Exchange Layer

外國輸出：工作機會
美國輸入：創造力、匯款、影響力、商業價值

(D) Ethical Redistribution Contract

避免剝削；保障人才雙向利益。

中文版

1. 問題背景

有些國家 AI 發達，有些國家沒有。
這造成 生產力不平等，但也產生新機會：

有 AI 的國家 → 人才過剩（失業風險）
無 AI 的國家 → 人才不足（產業升級困難）

轉為雙贏系統。

⸻

2. 解方：全球人才重新分配 GTR Model
	•	美國培養好的應屆畢業生 →
	•	經再訓練後 →
	•	分配到 沒有 AI 的國家 支援產業 →
	•	用遠距方式創造全球價值

同時解決：
	•	美國新鮮人的就業問題
	•	AI 低落國家的技術缺口

⸻

3. 創造者領導模式（Creator-Based Employment）

每一代都有 10–20% 的創意人才。
你的設計是：
	1.	在應屆畢業生中找出 創造者（Creators）
	2.	他們負責提點子、設計系統、開發概念
	3.	再配對 後端工程師（Builders）
	4.	組成完整 創意×工程團隊
	5.	讓其他國家的產業吸收這些工作量（Operators）

把「一個人才 → 變成一個團隊」

直接增加大量就業機會。

⸻

4. 三層 AI 時代團隊架構

Layer 1：創造者（Creator）

負責概念、模型、產品想法。

Layer 2：工程師（Builder）

負責 coding、AI、系統架構。

Layer 3：操作人才（Operator）

負責執行、維運、國際合作、遠距工作。

一個 Creator 可以創造 3–20 個工作機會

真正帶來「就業倍增效應」。

5. 為什麼這個模式可行？

（1）阻止 AI 造成失業

因為 AI 用來放大人類，不是取代人類。

（2）讓應屆畢業生重新站起來

每個層級都有工作。

（3）幫助無 AI 國家產業升級

（4）強化美國的全球競爭力與影響力

（5）形成可持續的跨國價值鏈

⸻

6. LORI 觀點

AI 的價值不是集中，而是重新分配。
人才是文明的核心資源，必須透過分工讓每一屆畢業生都有位置。

LORI 加上：
	•	GTEI（全球人才均衡指數）
	•	CBO（三層人才架構）
	•	全球價值交換層（工作輸出 × 創造力輸入）

這就變成完整的人才治理模型。
